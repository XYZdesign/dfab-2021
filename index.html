What is Digital Fabrication And Prototyping Fundamentals (EP1000)?

This is an elective module, organised around a self-directed project where students develop design thinking and prototyping skills. Computer Aided Design techniques, 3D printing and laser cutting methods are used to develop the prototype. In addition, microcontroller are used to add intelligence to our project.
